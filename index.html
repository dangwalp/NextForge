Articles

Below are a few articles that have truly intrigued my interest and expanded my understanding of various aspects of AI, machine learning, and language models. These readings have inspired much of my recent thinking and work:

  • What Are Multimodal Large Language Models? - Azure

    This article explores Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs), which I find fascinating for their ability to combine text, images, and other data types. The potential for MLLMs to revolutionize how AI interacts with the world is truly exciting.

  • WaveNet: A Generative Model for Raw Audio - DeepMind

    DeepMind’s WaveNet model caught my attention because it represents a major breakthrough in generative audio models. Its ability to produce natural-sounding speech from raw audio is a powerful example of AI’s creative potential.

    This article on unsupervised machine translation resonated with me due to its innovative approach to language translation without large labeled datasets. The potential to create fast, high-quality translations for a wider range of languages is impressive.
